Our Story

Same standards since 2017.

We started Yard Dog in 2017 with one truck, one trailer, and a stubborn belief that lawn care should feel personal. The first jobs were neighbors and friends. We mowed, we edged, we showed up when we said we would, and word got around Longview pretty quickly.

Almost a decade later, we still answer the phone, still walk every property, and still treat your yard like our own front lawn. The trucks are nicer and the crew is bigger, but the bar hasn't moved: do the work the way it's supposed to be done, charge a fair price, and leave the place better than we found it.

Today we serve Longview, Kilgore, Hallsville, Marshall, Tyler, and the rest of East Texas with weekly maintenance, full landscape installs, hardscaping, fertilization programs, leaf removal, and seasonal Christmas light installs. Whether it's a routine mow or a full backyard transformation, you get the same crew, the same standards, and the same straight answers every time.
