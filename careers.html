Join the Pack. We Are Always Looking for Good Hands.

Good pay, a paid day to prove it, and a crew that takes care of each other. Get on the short list and you get the call first.

  • Top-of-market pay. We pay above the going rate for good hands in East Texas, with room to earn more as you raise your game.
  • A paid trial day. Your first day running the gear is paid. No free tryouts.
  • A real crew. Steady work, good equipment, and teammates who carry their weight and look out for each other.
  • Room to climb. Show us what you can do and the ceiling comes off.

The application below takes about two minutes from your phone. Answer it straight. If you look like a fit, the next step is a quick call, then a paid trial day running the gear you tell us you can run.

Application

Get on the short list.

About two minutes from your phone. Be honest. It is the fastest way onto the list, and the fastest way off it if it is not true.

About you
A few yes/no basics
Are you legally authorized to work in the United States?*
Are you 18 years of age or older?*
Do you have a valid driver's license?*
Do you have reliable transportation to get to our shop or job sites every workday?*
This job means being outdoors in Texas heat and lifting up to 50 lbs repeatedly. Can you perform these essential job functions, with or without reasonable accommodation?*
Our crews generally work Monday through Friday and start early, around 6 AM, to beat the heat. Saturday is overtime as needed. Are you able to work that schedule?*
After a job offer is made, are you willing to consent to a background check and a motor vehicle record check?*
Have you been convicted of a felony or misdemeanor (other than a minor traffic offense) in the last 7 years?*

A "Yes" answer will not automatically disqualify you. Yard Dog Landscapes considers each applicant individually, looking at the nature of the offense, how long ago it occurred, and whether it relates to the duties of this job. We do not ask about arrests or charges that did not lead to a conviction. Any criminal-background check is run only after a conditional job offer, with your written consent.

Your experience

Do you have commercial mowing experience (running mowing routes or crews for a business, not just mowing your own yard)?*
How many years of paid landscaping, lawn care, or outdoor labor experience do you have?*
