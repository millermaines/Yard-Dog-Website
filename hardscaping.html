Overview

Hardscaping built for Longview & East Texas yards.

Hardscape installation Longview TX homeowners can actually count on starts with the ground itself. We dig the right depth, compact the right base, and pitch every patio so water moves where it should — not toward your foundation. Whether you need a flagstone path, a flagship paver patio, or a full backyard buildout with seating walls and a fire pit, our crew handles the layout, the materials, and the cleanup.

How much does a paver patio or hardscape cost in Longview, TX?

Paver patios and hardscape projects in the Longview area typically start around $3,000 and can run well into the five figures, depending on square footage, the materials you pick, and how much grading and base work the site needs. Every project is different, so the exact price comes from a free on-site quote — call (903) 844-6877 and we'll walk it with you.

What hardscaping services do you offer?

We build paver and stone patios, walkways, fire pits, seating walls, and outdoor living spaces across East Texas. We plan every hardscape around your yard's grade and drainage so it stays level and solid through our wet East Texas seasons.

How long does a hardscape project take?

Most residential patios and walkways take a few days to a couple of weeks, depending on the size of the project and how much base and grading work is involved. We give you a clear timeline in your written estimate before we start so there are no surprises.

Do you build hardscapes to handle East Texas drainage?

Yes. We set every patio and walkway on a properly compacted base and plan the grade so water runs away from your home, not toward it — critical in our clay-subsoil Gregg County ground. We're family-owned, fully insured, and hold a 5.0 rating across 111 Google reviews.
