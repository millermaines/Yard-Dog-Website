Local Knowledge

What we see in Kilgore yards.

Kilgore is just twelve miles southwest of our Longview shop — same county, same climate, same soil — and we're on properties here almost every day of the week. The town's known for the World's Richest Acre downtown and the Kilgore College Rangerettes, but for landscaping purposes what matters is that a lot of Kilgore neighborhoods have heavy tree canopy: mature pecans, post oaks, and loblolly pines that throw a lot of shade and a lot of leaf litter.

That canopy changes the grass calculus. Most of the older Kilgore neighborhoods are St. Augustine because Bermuda won't hold under that much shade, and fungal pressure runs high — brown patch in late summer, take-all root rot in spring on the lower ground. We run fertilization programs in Kilgore that lean toward slower-release nitrogen and watch sulfur levels closely, since acidic sandy soil under decades of pine needles tends to trend pH-low.

Yard Dog has Kilgore as one of our core routes — tight geography, same-day responsiveness, and a long list of clients we've held since 2017. If you're in Kilgore and need anything from weekly mowing to a full backyard rebuild, the answer is yes and the estimate usually comes back same-day.
