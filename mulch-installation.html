Frequently Asked Questions

How much does mulch installation cost in Longview, TX?

Most mulch refreshes in the Longview area run from about $300 to $1,200, depending on how many square feet of beds you have and the type of mulch you choose. Every yard is different, so the exact price comes from a free quote — call (903) 844-6877 and we'll measure your beds.

How much mulch do my beds need?

We install mulch at about one to one-and-a-half inches deep, which is the right depth to hold moisture and block weeds without smothering roots in our East Texas heat. We measure your beds and calculate the exact amount so you're not paying for more than you need.

What type of mulch is best for East Texas?

Hardwood and cedar mulches both do well in East Texas — they hold up to our heat and humidity and slow down weed growth. We'll recommend the type and color that fits your beds and the look you want when we walk the property.

How often should mulch be refreshed?

Most Longview beds benefit from a fresh mulch top-off once a year, usually in spring, to keep the color, moisture retention, and weed suppression working. Yard Dog is family-owned, fully insured, and serves Longview and all of Gregg County.
